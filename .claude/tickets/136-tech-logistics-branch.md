## Beschreibung

Tech-Branch für strategische Logistik-Optimierung. Cross-Track-Branch (kein eigener Specialist), aber für alle wertvoll.

Tech-Tree (Tier 1-5):

**Tier 1**: Construction-Management (Bau-Queue-Slots +1 T-094), Cargo-Optimization (Schiff-Cargo +10%)
**Tier 2**: Standardized-Construction (Bau-Zeit -10% Stack mit T-064), Trade-Route-Optimization (T-110 +1 Route)
**Tier 3**: Auto-Logistics (T-095 Auto-Routing unlocked), Multi-Planet-Coordination (Bau-Queue +2 Slots)
**Tier 4**: Galactic-Trade-Network (T-110 +5 Routes, max 10), Colonial-Expansion (T-101 Planet-Cap +1, max 7)
**Tier 5**: Imperial-Logistics (Bau-Queue +5 Slots, Bau-Zeit -25%), Frontier-Doctrine (T-101 Planet-Cap +3, max 10)
