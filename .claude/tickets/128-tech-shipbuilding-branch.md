## Beschreibung

Tech-Branch für Military-Specialist-Track (T-098). Unlockt Mark-Tier, neue Schiffsklassen, Build-Speed.

Tech-Tree (Tier 1-5):

**Tier 1**: Modular-Hull (Frigate/Destroyer Build-Cost -10%), Standardized-Components (Build-Time -10%)
**Tier 2**: Mk-II-Production (Voraussetzung Mk-II-Schiffe T-102), Cruiser-Class (Cruiser-Construction unlocked, falls nicht von Raumwerft-Level abhängig)
**Tier 3**: Battleship-Class (Battleship-Construction unlocked), Reactive-Armor-Tech (T-091 Reactive-Armor-Building)
**Tier 4**: Mk-III-Production, Carrier-Doctrine (Carrier-Klasse + Fighter-Squadrons), Targeting-Computer-Tech
**Tier 5**: Plasma-Coil-Tech (Plasma-Forge T-091 unlocked), Capital-Ship-Doctrine (Battleship-Mk-III-Boost +25% HP)
